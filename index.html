About

You name it, We make it.

  • 2013-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The story of HATKE began when few crazy folks from a B-Scholl had planned for their own college fest; it was that success of their college fest which they pulled it out on a grand scale within a 13days of hard work.

  • 2016

    An Agency is Born

    An idea of getting kick started with their own event management company which would be different from any other event mgmt company.and hence came with a different idea or a “Hatke” idea by naming their company as HATKE with its own tag line as the USP, "Memories to Rejoice"!!

  • 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    Relationships is the key to success for us, we give much importance in maintain relationships.A trust worthy team which will do everything & anything for the event to blossom out like flower.

  • June 2018

    Phase Two Expansion

    Customer is the king” this is long old tradition which we have inherited in our company we ensure that you are treated as the king, & see to it that the event goes on a grand – gala scale. With memories to be remembered for ever.
